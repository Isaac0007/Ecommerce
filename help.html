Frequently Asked Questions

🛒 How do I place an order?
Simply browse our products, click "Add to Cart" on items you want, then go to your cart and click "Checkout". Follow the prompts to enter your shipping information and payment details to complete your order.
💳 What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Mobile Money (MTN MoMo, Airtel Money), Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard), Bank Transfer, and Cash on Delivery (Kampala only).
🚚 How long does delivery take?
Kampala deliveries take 1-2 business days, while upcountry deliveries take 2-5 business days. Express delivery options are also available for faster shipping.
🔄 What is your return policy?
We offer a 7-day return policy for defective or damaged products. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Contact our support team to initiate a return.
🔧 Do you offer warranty on products?
Yes, all electronics come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Accessories have 6-month warranty.
📱 How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via SMS/email. You can use this number on our Track My Order page to see real-time updates.
🔐 Is my payment information secure?
Absolutely! We use SSL encryption and secure payment gateways to protect your financial information. We never store your full payment details on our servers.
📞 How do I contact customer support?
You can reach us via phone at +256 7631 00000, email at support@anontech.ug, or through our live chat feature. We're available Monday-Friday, 7AM-7PM.
💰 Can I change or cancel my order?
Orders can be modified or canceled within 1 hour of placement. Please contact our support team immediately with your order number.
⭐ How do I leave a product review?
After receiving your order, you'll receive an email with a link to review your purchased products. You can also log into your account and go to "My Orders" to leave reviews.
