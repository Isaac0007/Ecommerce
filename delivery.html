Shipping & Delivery Information

At AnonTech, we strive to deliver your orders quickly and safely. Below you'll find all the information you need about our delivery services.

📍 Delivery Areas

We currently deliver to all major cities and towns across Uganda, including Kampala, Jinja, Entebbe, Mbarara, Gulu, and more. Remote areas may take additional 1-2 business days.

🚚 Delivery Options & Costs

Delivery Method Delivery Time Cost
Standard Delivery (Kampala) 1-2 business days UGX 15,000
Standard Delivery (Upcountry) 2-5 business days UGX 25,000 - 45,000
Express Delivery (Kampala) Same day (order before 12pm) UGX 30,000
Express Delivery (Upcountry) Next business day UGX 60,000
Free Shipping Standard delivery time FREE on orders over UGX 500,000

📦 Order Processing

Orders are processed within 24 hours on business days (Monday-Friday). Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed the next business day.

📱 Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email/SMS with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on our Track My Order page.

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to unforeseen circumstances
  • Please ensure someone is available to receive the package at the delivery address
  • You will receive a call from our delivery agent before arrival
  • If you are not available, a rescheduling fee may apply
